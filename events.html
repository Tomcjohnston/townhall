Linking between what’s happened in the past and re-imaging the space for the future. The Town Hall are staging community events before its re-open.

26 October

Location
7pm - Late
Free Entry

Empty Lot Rave

Enjoy the sun, the grass and music this spring for the first event in preparation for the re-opening of the Town Hall. Join us at this free family event featuring some of Wellington’s best Dj’s and bands for a night you won't want to forget. This event is held in the empty lot on Tasman Street.

Location
7pm - Late

Zine Fest

Bring along your zines, pens, paper, patterns, scissors and cash for the Wellington Zine Fest . Featuring some of Wellington's small independent publishers this is your chance to see some of the creative side of Wellington.
